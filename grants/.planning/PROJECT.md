# Global Grants — Open to All (Location-Agnostic)

**Goal:** Secure €100k–€500k in non-dilutive funding for OpenClaw stack and AI automation R&D.

**Eligibility:** No geographic restriction — you can apply from Nigeria or anywhere. Most require:
- Legal entity (can be sole proprietorship, or register a US LLC/EU company for some)
- Bank account (international wire)
- CV/biosketch of PI (you)
- Project proposal with budget

**Target grant types:**
- Open-source software funding (corporations, foundations)
- AI research & development (government agencies, EU)
- Web3/crypto grants (for decentralized AI)
- Global innovation prizes/competitions
- Impact-focused foundations (education, climate, health — if we apply AI to these domains)

**Approach:** Cast wide net, tailor proposals per program, emphasize open-source nature and social impact (AI for good, productivity).
