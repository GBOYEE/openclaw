# 03-03: Acquire First Consulting Clients

**Epic**: Consulting Launch

## Goal

Land 1–2 paying consulting contracts ($5k+ total) within 8 weeks.

## Acceptance Criteria

- At least one signed SOW and deposit payment
- Projects delivered on time with positive testimonial
- Pipeline of 3 additional leads in funnel

## Tasks

1. Post on LinkedIn/Twitter: "Open for AI automation consulting" with link to one-pager
2. Email existing network (former colleagues, open source contributors) with offer
3. Reach out to 10 small startups that could benefit from automation (e.g., e-commerce, crypto, SaaS ops)
4. Offer 20% discount for first 2 clients to build testimonials
5. Deliver first audit/project with high touch; ask for referral

## Dependencies

03-01, 03-02

## Time Estimate

Ongoing; 2–4 hours/week
