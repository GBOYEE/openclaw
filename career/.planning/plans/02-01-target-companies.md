# 02-01: Identify Target Companies

**Epic**: Job Acquisition

## Goal

Create a prioritized list of 50 companies where an "AI Automation Engineer" would be valuable.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Spreadsheet (or YAML) with: company name, URL, contact (hiring manager/ founder), reason to target, remote policy, tech stack clues
- 20 startups, 20 mid-size, 10 large (quant/FAANG)
- Each has clear fit: they use AI agents, automation, or need internal tooling

## Tasks

1. Search: AI infrastructure, agent frameworks, automation SaaS, crypto trading firms, DevOps robotics companies
2. Leverage:YC, AngelList, LinkedIn, well-known remote-first tech companies
3. For each, note: recent funding, tech blog posts mentioning agents/automation
4. Prioritize by: remote, salary bands, Stack Overflow jobs, Lever postings
5. Export to CSV and add to memory

## Dependencies

01-01 (brand helps targeting)

## Time Estimate

4 hours
