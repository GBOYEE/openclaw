# 05-01: Create Video Course

**Epic**: Passive Scale

## Goal

Package GSD and AI automation knowledge into a premium video course ($297 one-time).

## Acceptance Criteria

- 6 modules, 30+ lessons (5–15 min each), total ~4 hours
- Topics: GSD philosophy, building agent-core, securing automation, deploying with Docker, scaling to SaaS
- Hosted on Podia/Teachable or Gumroad (with payments & delivery)
- Sales page with testimonials, money-back guarantee
- 50+ sales in first 6 months

## Tasks

1. Outline curriculum (intro → advanced)
2. Record first 3 modules using OBS (screen + voice)
3. Edit videos (remove mistakes, add intros/outros)
4. Upload to platform; set pricing
5. Create sales page; connect Stripe/PayPal
6. Promote via blog, Twitter, LinkedIn; offer limited-time discount

## Dependencies

01-04 (blog content can fuel course)

## Time Estimate

40 hours recording/editing + 8 hours setup
