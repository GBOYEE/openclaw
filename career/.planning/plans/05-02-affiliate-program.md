# 05-02: Launch Affiliate Program

**Epic**: Passive Scale

## Goal

Generate 15% of product revenue via affiliates (developers, tech influencers).

## Acceptance Criteria

- Affiliate system (via Stripe or dedicated platform like Lemon Squeezy)
- 15% recurring commission for referred subscription customers
- Affiliate portal (dashboard, referral links, earnings)
- Recruit 10 affiliates (tech YouTubers, indie hackers)
- Track conversions; pay monthly

## Tasks

1. Choose platform: Stripe Connect or Lemon Squeezy (handles taxes, payouts)
2. Set up affiliate terms and commission structure
3. Create affiliate signup page
4. Prepare marketing kit: banners, email templates, sample tweets
5. Reach out to potential affiliates with personalized invite + high commission
6. Monitor fraud; optimize payouts

## Dependencies

04-04 (product must exist)

## Time Estimate

8 hours
