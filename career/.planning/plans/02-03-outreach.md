# 02-03: Systematic Outreach

**Epic**: Job Acquisition

## Goal

Directly engage with founders and engineering managers at target companies to get interviews.

## Acceptance Criteria

- 50 personalized connection requests/messages on LinkedIn + Twitter
- 20 emails to hiring managers (templates + custom research notes)
- 5+ informational interviews scheduled
- 2+ technical interviews secured

## Tasks

1. Draft 3 outreach templates (value-first: "I built X that could help Y")
2. For each target company, identify 1–2 contacts (LinkedIn, company site)
3. Send messages in batches (5/day) to avoid spam; track responses
4. Follow up after 4 days if no reply
5. Prepare 30-second elevator pitch and 5-minute project demo for calls

## Dependencies

02-01, 02-02

## Time Estimate

4 hours (ongoing weekly)
