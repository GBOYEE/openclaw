# FairHire — Bias‑Free AI Recruitment Assistant

**Problem:** Traditional hiring tools perpetuate discrimination (gender, race, age). Candidates from underrepresented groups get filtered out unknowingly.

**Solution:** FairHire — an open‑source recruitment assistant that:
- Analyzes job descriptions for biased language (using word‑embeddings)
- Anonymizes resumes (removes names, photos, dates) while preserving skills
- Scores candidate–job fit with explainable AI (SHAP values)
- Audits decisions for disparate impact (statistical parity)
- Generates compliance reports (EEOC, NYC AI hiring law)

**Target grants:** Mozilla Trustworthy AI, Ford Foundation (technology for justice), Google AI for Good (if partnered with nonprofit), Fast Grants (bias/fairness).

**Success (12 months):**
- 100+ companies using FairHire (mostly SMEs)
- 10k+ resumes processed
- Show 20% increase in diversity of shortlists in pilot studies
- Publish research paper on audit methodology

**Tech:** Python, FastAPI, spaCy for NER, SHAP for explainability, PostgreSQL, React frontend.

**Why fundable:** Addresses pressing societal issue; open source builds trust; aligns with responsible AI mandates.
