# AutoSME — AI Automation for African Small Businesses

**Problem:** Small businesses in Africa struggle with manual inventory, ad‑hoc scheduling, and fragmented sales channels. They can't afford expensive SaaS. Manual work consumes hours weekly.

**Solution:** An affordable, mobile‑first AI automation platform that:
- Connects WhatsApp Business API → auto‑reply orders, track inventory
- Schedules deliveries, staff shifts via simple forms
- Generates weekly sales reports automatically
- Uses agent‑core for planning, automation‑engine for scheduling, and a lightweight React‑Native/Flutter frontend

**Target grants:**
- Tony Elumelu Foundation ($5k–$50k) — African entrepreneurship
- Gitcoin Grants (matching pool) — open‑source impact
- Mozilla Economic Opportunity Fund — AI for inclusive growth
- Google AI for Science (if we add data‑driven impact metrics) — $500k–$3M

**Success metrics (12 months):**
- 500+ active SME users across Nigeria/Kenya/Ethiopia
- 80% time saved on admin tasks (survey)
- $100k GMV processed through platform (commission revenue)
- Open source core with 200+ GitHub stars

**Tech stack:** Python (agent‑core, automation‑engine), FastAPI backend, SQLite/PostgreSQL, WhatsApp Business API, Twilio, Tailwind/Flutter.

**Why this wins:** Direct impact on African economic growth, aligns with funder priorities (inclusion, jobs, AI for good), built with GSD discipline (fast, reliable, open source).
