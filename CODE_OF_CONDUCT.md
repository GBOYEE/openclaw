# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our
community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body
size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender
identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status,
nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual
identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming,
diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Demonstrating empathy and kindness
- Being respectful of differing opinions
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Accepting responsibility and apologizing for mistakes
- Focusing on the best for the community

Unacceptable behavior:

- Sexualized language or imagery
- Trolling, insults, derogatory comments
- Harassment (public or private)
- Publishing private information without permission
- Other unprofessional conduct

## Scope

This Code applies in all community spaces and when representing the community
in public (official email, social media, events).

## Enforcement

Report violations to security@example.com. All complaints will be reviewed
promptly and confidentially.

## Enforcement Guidelines

1. **Correction** — Private written correction.
2. **Warning** — Warning with potential temporary interaction ban.
3. **Temporary Ban** — Temporary ban from community interaction.
4. **Permanent Ban** — Permanent ban for sustained violations.
